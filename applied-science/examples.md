# Example Clients

Use these during development and in your demo. Each represents a realistic scenario a sales rep might gather in a 20-minute discovery call. They include details that are intentionally a little messy — just like real client conversations.

---

## Client A — NovaDash (mid-market SaaS, freemium)

*"We're a project management tool, about 400 employees. Most of our growth comes from organic and paid search landing on our homepage. We convert free trials but the real money is trial-to-paid, which happens inside the product."*

| Page | DAU | Baseline conversion | Revenue per conversion | Target lift |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Homepage | 9 400 | 4.8% (free trial start) | $0 (freemium — paid conversion happens later) | 10% |
| Pricing page | 1 100 | 2.3% (trial start from pricing) | $79/month | 15% |
| Sign-up form | 4 500 | 61% (form completion) | $0 | 5% |

Win rate assumption: 20% · Significance: 95% · Power: 80%

> *"Also — we're planning a brand refresh in about 8 weeks. Should we even be running experiments on the current design?"*

## Client B — Thornfield & Co (enterprise e-commerce, apparel)

*"We do about $40M a year online. Homepage drives product discovery, but our real drop-off is between product page and checkout. We sell across the US and Canada."*

| Page | DAU | Baseline conversion | Revenue per conversion | Target lift |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Homepage | 52 000 | 2.1% (click-through to product) | — | 8% |
| Product page | 22 000 | 4.6% (add to cart) | — | 10% |
| Checkout | 6 800 | 71% (order completion) | $185 avg order | 5% |

Win rate assumption: 25% · Significance: 95% · Power: 80%

> *"Our traffic in November and December is roughly 3× normal because of the holidays. Does that affect how we think about test timing?"*

## Client C — Stackform (mid-market B2B, dev tooling)

*"We sell to engineering teams, so our traffic is relatively small but highly qualified. We care about demo requests — that's our main funnel entry point. Deals close somewhere between 60 and 90 days after the demo."*

| Page | DAU | Baseline conversion | Revenue per conversion | Target lift |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Homepage | 1 800 | 3.1% (clicks to demo page) | — | 12% |
| Demo request page | 620 | 7.4% (demo booked) | $4 200 avg deal × 14% close rate | 15% |

Win rate assumption: 15% · Significance: 95% · Power: 80%

> *"The $4,200 is average contract value but we only close about 14% of demos, and it takes 60–90 days to know if a demo converted. Does that change how you model revenue impact?"*
